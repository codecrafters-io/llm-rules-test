## Rule
- All section headings must be level 3 (###).
- No level 1 (#) or level 2 (##) headings allowed.

## Good examples
- `### Tests`
- `### Explanation`
- `### Notes`

## Bad examples
- `# Title` (level 1 heading)
- `## Introduction` (level 2 heading)
- `#### Subsection` (level 4 heading)
